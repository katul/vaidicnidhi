Our Services

Pooja

Connect with a Pune pandit for your puja and receive guidance throughout the process & experience the rich traditions of Maharashtra with a pooja performed by a Pune pandit.Make your next pooja unforgettable with the guidance and blessings of a learned pandit.

Path

Elevate your spiritual journey with a melodious path conducted by a Pune pandit, filling your home with divine chants.Learn from the experience and knowledge of a Pune pandit as they guide you through a traditional katha or path.

Hawan

Hawan, performed by a Pune Pandit, is a sacred ritual involving offerings to a holy fire. The Pandit, an expert in Vedic traditions, starts by preparing the fire and altar. He chants special mantras from the Vedas while adding items like ghee, grains, and herbs into the fire.

Jaap

Jaap, led by a Pune Pandit, is a spiritual practice where sacred mantras are repeated many times.These mantras, from ancient texts, are chanted to bring divine blessings and positive energy. Repeating the mantras helps to calm the mind and bring peace to everyone involved.
