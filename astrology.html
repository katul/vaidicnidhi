The Different Branches of Astrology


Vedic Astrology (Jyotish): Originating from ancient India, Vedic astrology focuses on the Moon’s position and uses the sidereal zodiac. It emphasizes karmic lessons and life purpose. Western Astrology: Predominantly using the tropical zodiac, Western astrology emphasizes the Sun's position at birth and includes psychological elements to analyze character traits and life events. Chinese Astrology: Based on a lunar calendar, Chinese astrology categorizes individuals into twelve animal signs that cycle every twelve years. Each sign carries specific characteristics and influences. Numerology: This practice assigns meaning to numbers associated with a person’s name and birth date, revealing insights into personality and life path. Tarot and Divination: While not astrology in the strictest sense, tarot reading and other divination methods often complement astrological practices, providing intuitive guidance and clarity.
