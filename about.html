About Us

We offer the guidance of highly qualified Pandits.

They have dedicated themselves to the study of Vedic scriptures at renowned Pathashalas and possess the experience to ensure a beautiful and spiritually fulfilling puja ceremony.

An esteemed initiative by Pune and Shri Ram kunj katha mandap Ayodhya Foundation, stands as the premier destination for booking pandits for both home and temple poojas in the vibrant city of Pune. With a rich history of facilitating sacred rituals, we take pride in our extensive network of experienced vedic pandits who ensure the seamless execution of every ceremony. Having conducted countless ceremonies with utmost dedication and authenticity, we cordially invite you to experience our exceptional services firsthand.
