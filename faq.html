Frequently Asked Questions

A Pandit is a learned scholar who acts as your guide during religious ceremonies. They possess in-depth knowledge of Vedic scriptures and rituals, ensuring the ceremonies are conducted authentically and meaningfully.

Would it be possible to make a partial payment in advance to secure the booking for the puja?

Yes, you can make a partial payment as a token advance to secure your booking. Following the ceremony, you can settle the remaining balance conveniently. You have the option to pay in cash directly to our priests or you can choose UPI for an online transfer.

What Should I Do When Pandit Ji Do Not Arrive On Time?

If pandit ji doesn't arrive on time you can contact to us through phone or fill in form given below.

Can the puja ceremony be conducted in a language that my guests will understand?

Absolutely! We understand the importance of ensuring everyone feels included and can participate meaningfully in your puja ceremony.Just let us know your preferences, and we'll ensure the puja is conducted in a way that fosters understanding and connection for everyone involved.

Are there any additional charges for having the puja & Hawan ceremony?

The cost of your puja and havan ceremony is designed to be transparent and straightforward. All chanrges will be included in to your puja package.

Can you give me an estimated timeframe for a puja ceremony?

It depends which puja you are organising. for Ex: Satnarayan katha takes 2-3 hours with all set and katha while Vastu shanti puja may take 2 hours to complete.

Would it be possible to conect with pandit ji before puja?

Yes, you can talk directly to our dedicated panditji after booking to finalize puja muhrut and timings.
